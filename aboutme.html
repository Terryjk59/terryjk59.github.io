About MeTerry

Hello, my name is Terry. I am a freshman at URI majoring in Film and media. I’m a goofy photographer trying to learn new things. I’ve worked as a freelance photographer and as a secondary photographer at an advertising firm before coming to URI, now that I’m here I mostly keep my photographic work limited to my course work.

Besides being a shutter bug, I love sitting inside gaming or editing. As a photographer I’ve spent more time editing pictures than taking them.
Other than editing I’m also into video games. If I’m not work or editing, I’m playing video games. Other than that, I’m a pretty normal guy who likes taking pictures.

Fun fact: As much as I love taking pictures and capturing moments I hate being in front of the lens, I don’t have too many pictures of myself.


I have been taking pictures and vides for about 6 years now and still no matter how good I get there is always room to grow. For that growth I decided to learn coding… trying to at the very least.

I’d like to consider myself a bit of a movie buff but every now and then I come across someone who introduces me to some new director, actor, creator or whatever and gets me into some new genre of films I never thought to explore.


If you dear reader have some interesting film I should watch please let me know in the comment box I shall soon learn to code.
